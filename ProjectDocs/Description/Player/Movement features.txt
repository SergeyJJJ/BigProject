* Character Movement
 + Can change vertical speed.
 + Can change horizontal speed.
 + Can change extent of movement smoothing.

* Character Jumping
 + Can change jump velocity
 + Can change time during which the player can jump after he fell form the ground.
 + Can change time during which player can press jump button before touching the ground, and jump will be performed
 + Can change extent of jump height controll.
 + Can customize "ground check" parameters that determine if player is touching the ground and what actually is ground.
